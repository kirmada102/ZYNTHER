About Us

A health helper concept built to feel gentle, glowing, and useful.

orenva is imagined as a soft digital companion that helps people care for themselves with less pressure and more clarity.

Founder & CEO

Geet Patil

Registered name: Orenva Health Pvt Ltd

Geet works on business development and strategy and brand building with 5+ years of exp in MedTech industry building award winning innovative products.

Our mission

Make health support feel kinder. We believe a wellness website can guide people with warmth, visual calm, and approachable AI help instead of cold dashboards and complicated language.

Our visual idea

The circular robot icon represents emotional ease, light monitoring, and a small spark of smart guidance. Its white to purple gradient shifts every second to keep the brand feeling alive.
